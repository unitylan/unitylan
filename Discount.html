Frequently Asked Questions

Who is eligible?

Registered NGOs, NPOs and community organisations with proof of registration are eligible. Priority to active organisations serving communities. Optional services like advanced SEO, and ongoing updates are paid add-ons you choose after the site is delivered.

Is the website really free?

Yes — the build is free. We may request verification documents. Hosting, domain or premium add-ons (if requested) are handled separately and may incur fees — we'll confirm before proceeding.

How long does it take?

Typical turnaround is 7–14 business days from approval of content and assets, depending on queue and the complexity of the site.
