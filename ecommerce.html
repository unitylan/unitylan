eCommerce Features & Integrations

Explore the powerful features and seamless integrations we offer in our eCommerce solutions to make your online store stand out.

Product Management

Efficiently manage your entire product catalog, from adding new products to organizing categories, handling stock levels, and updating pricing details—all from an easy-to-use dashboard.

  • Bulk product import/export
  • Category and tag management
  • Inventory tracking and stock alerts
  • Product variations (size, color, etc.)

Payment Gateway Integration

Accept payments securely with integrated gateways like Peach Payments, Yoco, PayFlex, and more. Enable your customers to pay easily and confidently, with support for multiple currencies and payment methods.
